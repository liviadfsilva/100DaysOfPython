The Best Horror Movies According to Lily

My Top 5 Movies of All Time — Alphabetical Order

He's Out There

"I've been watching you for a long time."

Super creepy. It left me jaw-dropped and staring off into space at the end.

Psycho

"Well, a boy's best friend is his mother."

One of the first classic movies I ever watched. Absolutely loved the plot twist.

Scream

"What's your favorite scary movie?"

Ghostface is one of my favorit slashers ever. I love how cliché the movies are.

The Shining

"I'm not gonna hurt you. I'm just gonna bash your brains in."

This is my go-to movie when I'm bored. I'm a sucker for both the mbook and the movie. Amazing story!

The Witch

"Wouldst thou like to live deliciously?"

I love the lore surrounding witchcraft. Black Philip was my favorite — he totally stole the thunder.
